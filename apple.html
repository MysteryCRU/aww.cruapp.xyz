CRUapp on iOS

  1. Send your UDID to wasaequreshi@gmail.com
  2. Once registered, you will receive and email confirming your device and a password for HockeyApp
  3. Go to https://rink.hockeyapp.net/users/sign_in
  4. Use the login and password for the HockeyApp account provided in the confirmation email
  5. Install any HockeyApp provisioning if required. (Optional, may or may not show up)
  6. In the Dashboard, click on "myApp"
  7. Click the "Install" button
  8. The application should be installed on your device
